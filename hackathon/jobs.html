HOW CAN A JOB HELP YOU EARN MONEY?

What is a job? A job is a position where you do work and you get paid for however much work you complete, or however many hours you work.

How do you get a job? The legal age to work in Texas is 14, so can get a job as long as you're 14 years old. You can get a job by talking to adults to see if they need any help at their workplace, you can google for specific jobs you are interested in and apply online. You will need to make a resume, in which you list all the experience you have, skills you have, and your grades.

Why do you need to get a job? You need to get a job because it provides a steady source of money for you to live off of so you can have more freedom with your money!
