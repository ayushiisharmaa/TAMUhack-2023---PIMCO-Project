WHAT IS A BUDGET?

What is a budget? A budget is a plan to make sure you have a little bit of money for everything: snacks, games, toys, and school.

How do you make a budget? Create a plan, count how much money you have, and split the money up. Put more money in your needs and less money in your wants to be smart with money!

Why do you need a budget? A budget helps you save your money so you have money for everything: Food, Fun, and For School!

What is the 50/30/20 rule? The 50/30/20 rule is to divide your money into three categories: spending 50% on needs such as toilet paper and food, 30% on wants such as toys and candy, and 20% on savings.

Why should you follow the 50/30/20 rule? The 50/30/20 is a type of budget that helps you make sure you don't waste money on nonessential items.
