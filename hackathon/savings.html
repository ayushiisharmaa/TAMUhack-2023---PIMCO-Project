HOW TO SAVE

What is savings? Savings include some money you set aside for emergencies like when you get sick and have to go to the doctor or the hospital.

How do you save? Every time you get money, you set a percent of money aside in your savings envelope! You never touch this envelope unless its an emergency.

Why is it good to save? Savings help you have some extra money for things you do not plan for, like emergencies!

Saving is a really good habit because it enables us to understand how much money we need to spend and it prevents excessive spending on things that we don't really need.

Saving can be done in many ways: you can save at home in a piggy bank, or in a real bank where your money grows due to interest.
